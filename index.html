Fix

Add unique, keyword-rich meta descriptions (120-160 chars) to every page. Add og:title, og:description, og:image, og:url to all pages. Add twitter:card and twitter:title tags. Start with the 5 core service and landing pages.

High

Missing and broken heading hierarchy across key pages

Search engines and AI systems use heading structure to understand page topics. Broken hierarchy undermines this.

Evidence:

/salary-packaging/: No H1 or H2. Jumps straight to H3 and H6 tags
/novated-leasing/: H1 present but skips H2, jumps to H3
/fleet-management/: No clear H1 detected. Uses H3 and H6
/about-us/: H1 present ("About Us") but thin hierarchy below

H6 tags used for content subheadings on salary packaging page (should be H2/H3)
+
Fix

Add keyword-rich H1 to homepage (e.g. "Salary Packaging & Novated Leasing for Australian Employers"). Fix salary packaging page: add H1, convert H6s to H3s. Ensure clean H1 → H2 → H3 nesting on all pages.
